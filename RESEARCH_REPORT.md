# Boise Business Websites — Online Presence Research Report

Researched: 2026-07-11
Method: Web search (`"<business name> Boise Idaho"` plus targeted follow-ups) for each of the 23 businesses, checking for an owned/dedicated website, Google Business Profile strength, Facebook/Instagram activity, and Yelp review volume/rating. No files were modified, moved, or deleted — this is a research-only report.

**Bottom line: 0 of 23 businesses have a real, owned website.** All "found" presences are third-party (Yelp, Facebook, Instagram, directories, delivery apps, press features). Classification therefore turned on how *strong* the substitute presence is (review volume/rating, multi-platform activity, press coverage) versus thin/fragmented/unclaimed listings.

- **REMOVE candidates: 5** — strong existing digital footprint (high-volume/high-rating reviews, multi-platform social, or a food-truck model where roaming social + delivery/locator apps function as their "website").
- **KEEP candidates: 18** — no real website and thin, fragmented, unclaimed, or unverifiable online presence; good outreach targets.

No businesses appeared closed or defunct. One (Kodiak Taxidermy Studio) had unusually thin search results and could not be fully verified — flagged below.
